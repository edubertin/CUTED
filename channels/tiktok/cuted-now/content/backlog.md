# CUTED Now Content Backlog

Use this backlog for candidate posts before rendering or publishing.

## Status Labels

- `idea`: raw topic or source.
- `selected`: approved for CUTED processing.
- `rendered`: final MP4 exists.
- `ready`: caption and metadata reviewed.
- `posted`: live on TikTok.
- `retired`: not worth publishing.
